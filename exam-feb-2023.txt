Scenario

The following scenario serves as the basis for all of the following tasks.

A family-run screw manufacturer (a medium enterprise) that is a world market leader in a
specialized type of wood screws has come up with a new idea to incorporate sensors into
their screws. The sensors will be able to detect when a screw is being over-stressed, losing
pressure, or experiencing any other issues, and will send out an alert to the relevant parties.

The company, which has been in business for several decades, has built a reputation for pro-
ducing high-quality and reliable screws that are used in a wide range of industries, from con-
struction and manufacturing to aerospace and defense. However, as the demand for their
screws has grown, the company has become increasingly concerned about the potential for
screws to fail in the field, leading to costly downtime and repairs. When asking companies,
which suffered from costly downtime and repairs, 90% of those customers reported that they
would pay up to 100x the prices of regular screws for an improved screw.

At the same time, an American researcher published a paper on a novel micro-sensor tech-
nology (MiSe), which received very little attention yet. So, to address the company issue, the
company's management team has decided to invest in the development of MiSe, so that it
can be integrated into the screws. The sensors will be able to detect when a screw is ap-
proaching its maximum load capacity and will send an alert to the relevant parties, such as
maintenance crews or engineers, so that they can take action before the screw fails.

The company is working closely with a team of engineers and developers to design and test
the sensors, which will be small, low-power, and highly reliable. The sensors will be able to
communicate with other devices, such as smartphones or laptops, via Bluetooth or other
wireless technologies, so that the data can be analyzed in real-time. The system can also be
configured, depending on the risk a company is willing to take.

The company sees this new technology as a way to further differentiate their product and
also to increase customer satisfaction. They also hope that this technology will help prevent
accidents and increase safety. Finally, they want to include a hidden feature to track cus-
tomer usage data. The implementation of this new technology will take a few months but the
company is confident that the added value of the sensors will pay off in the long run.

The company plans to offer this new sensor-enabled screw as an upgrade option to its exist-
ing customers and also to market it as a new product. They also plan to patent the technol-
ogy to protect their intellectual property.
